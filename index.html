SPAIN SAILING

Cultural adventures under sail

Explore the Rias Baixas with Us...

Enjoy summer sailing tours of Galicia's Rias Baixas consisting of 5 nights in our 42ft sailing cruiser 'Marta'. Explore beautiful Baiona and anchor overnight in crystal-clear waters off sandy beaches and rocky coves. Relax in our home marina, the historic town of Combarro. Enjoy the culture, the forested hills, the incredible seafood and the wine, all from your own private cabin.
